## What This Is

Smoking Tigers AI (ST:AI) is an **AI-powered venture studio operating system** — built in production, under real constraints, by Smoking Tigers Media.

It started as an internal Chief-of-Staff intelligence layer. It is being intentionally evolved into a **productized platform for creator-led ventures and professional communities**.

The underlying insight: creative organizations don't fail because of lack of talent. They fail because of **coordination decay** — decisions buried in meetings, context locked in people's heads, projects drifting without ownership. ST:AI solves that without sacrificing privacy, autonomy, or governance.

## The Venture Studio Angle

The expanded model positions Smoking Tigers not just as a media company, but as a **venture studio** — with ST:AI as both its operating infrastructure and its first marketable product.

**What that means in practice:**

- ST:AI runs the studio's internal operations (meetings → decisions → memory → action)
- The same system is packaged and delivered to portfolio companies, creator communities, and professional orgs
- Smoking Tigers earns both from **operations** (internal leverage) and **service delivery** (external clients)
- Portfolio projects contribute IP, revenue points, and community — creating a multi-sided flywheel

This is a studio model with **broader appeal** because the product being built is also the infrastructure being proven.

## The Community → Capital Strategy

From prior strategy discussion with Frank:

**It starts with community.** The Q1 membership roster is a curated network of professionals — creators, technologists, operators, and investors — who are already operating within a shared governance model.

**The membership roster drives capital resource allocation:**
- Members contribute through Revenue Points (RP) — a tracked contribution currency with USD-equivalent face value
- Projects funded by the community earn RP, not just cash — aligning incentives across a contributor economic model
- Membership quality determines deal quality; the roster is the moat

**Flow:**
```
Community (Q1 roster) → Project Pipeline → RP Investment → IP Creation → Revenue → Distribution
```

Smoking Tigers sits at the center of this flow as **studio + platform operator**.

---

## Revenue Points Model (Brief)

Revenue Points are the internal contribution currency:
- Purchased like tokens at an arcade — real USD value
- Earned through contributions (content, code, production, strategy)
- Redeemable against project revenue
- Tracked via governance repo + Notion

This creates a **dual-currency operating model**: cash for operations, RP for contribution tracking and long-term alignment. Clean separation between working capital and equity-equivalent participation.
