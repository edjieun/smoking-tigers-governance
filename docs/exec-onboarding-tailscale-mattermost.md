## Step 1: Install Tailscale

Tailscale is what lets your device securely reach our private server. You won't be able to access Mattermost without it.

**Download:** [tailscale.com/download](https://tailscale.com/download)
Available for iOS, Android, Mac, and Windows.

After installing, **do not log in yet** — Ed will send you an invite link to join the private network. Use that link, not a personal account.

Once connected, Tailscale runs quietly in the background. You don't need to think about it after setup.

## Step 3: Find and Use Your Assigned Agent

Your AI assistant lives in Mattermost as a direct message contact.

**To find it:**
1. Log into Mattermost
2. Click **"Direct Messages"** in the left sidebar
3. Look for your assigned agent by name (Ed will confirm which one is yours)
4. Start typing — it will respond

**What your agent can do:**
- Answer questions and help you think through problems
- Draft emails, summaries, or documents
- Look things up and synthesize information
- Help you prep for meetings or decisions

Talk to it like you'd talk to a smart assistant. No special commands needed.
