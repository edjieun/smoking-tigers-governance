## 🛠️ The Podcast Spec (Draft 1.0)

### Glossary of Terms (So we don't use lazy buzzwords)

- **Solarpunk (Operationalized):** Not just solar panels and plants on buildings. It is the practical study and storytelling of decentralized, ecological, high-tech self-governance.
    
- **Anti-Idiocracy:** Content designed to demand active cognitive engagement from the listener. It rejects clickbait, outrage loops, and simplified binary debates, focusing instead on structural, system-level problem-solving.
    
- **Social Capital Host Style:** A hosting persona modeled after Dr. Dan Martin—disarmingly warm, deeply researched, metric-driven, and focused on trust-building, cooperation, and community resilience.

## 🛠️ The Podcast Spec (Draft 2.0)

### 1. The Operational Model: The Media Cooperative

- **The Structure:** A platform cooperative. Hosts, producers, and key contributors own fractional shares of the intellectual property (IP) they help create.
    
- **The Brand Strategy:** **The "Trojan Horse."** You are moving away from the "Solarpunk" aesthetic brand (which can feel like a subculture of "plants on skyscrapers" or niche activism) and moving toward **Systemic Optimism** or **Human-Scale Futures**.
    
- **The Revenue Engine:** _Symbiotic Recapture._ You will actively seek monetization from YouTube, standard ad networks, and sponsors. You are capturing the capital of "surveillance capitalism" and immediately routing it to fractional payouts for the creators and community-led regenerative projects.
    

### 2. The Host Dynamic: "The Translators"

- **The Model:** A dual-host, rotating model.
    
- **The Persona:** Think of it as **The Academic + The Pragmatic Maverick**. One host holds the deep system-level or scientific expertise (the "Dan Martin" archetype: data-driven, focused on social capital, warmth, and metrics); the other is the "translator" who brings the energy, the analogies, and keeps the conversation grounded in everyday reality.
    

### 3. The Listener Persona: "The Maverick Researcher"

- **Starting State:** Disillusioned with mainstream doom-scrolling and lazy, conspiratorial "do your research" rabbit holes. They have a healthy distrust of centralized authority but are exhausted by cynicism.
    
- **Target Behavior:** They don't just want to listen; they want to _interact_. They watch on YouTube, join a community forum/Discord, and actively look for projects (apps, local organizing, ecological actions) to join.
    

## 📋 The Guest Sourcing & Vetting Protocol (Draft 1.0)

Since this is a cooperative exercise, here is a draft matrix of parameters to help you and your co-op partners vet potential "Thought Leaders" and "Builders."

### The "Anti-Idiocracy" Guest Filter

To keep the show practical, engaging, and high-integrity, every guest should be evaluated across these four criteria before booking:

```
                  [ 1. SYSTEMIC COGNITION ]
                             │
                             ├─► Does this guest analyze structures, 
                             │   not just individual behaviors?
                             │
                  [ 2. THE "READ-THE-BOOK" PASS ]
                             │
                             ├─► Are they willing to actually read the 
			                 │   featured text and find points of                                       tension?
                             │
                  [ 3. PROVEN BIAS-TO-ACTION ]
                             │
                             ├─► Have they built something real? 
                             │   (An app, a community trust, a model)
                             │
                  [ 4. CHARISMATIC FLUIDITY ]
                             │
	                             ├─► Can they explain complex social                                        dynamics  without academic jargon?
```

### Guest Vetting Matrix

|**Parameter**|**The Ideal Candidate**|**Red Flags (Do Not Book)**|
|---|---|---|
|**Systemic Cognition**|Understands how resources, technology, and community needs interact.|Blames "bad individuals" or relies on simple moralizing.|
|**The "Read-the-Book" Pass**|Willing to read the designated book, find where they _disagree_ with it, and discuss it deeply.|Wants to do a standard press junket to pitch their own product without engaging in the host's thesis.|
|**Proven Bias-to-Action**|Has actively participated in or built a "human-scale" project (e.g., a coop, an open-source tool, a local mutual-aid network).|A pure theorist, sci-fi worldbuilder, or venture capitalist who only talks in "what-ifs."|
|**Charismatic Fluidity**|Can use humor, storytelling, and vivid metaphors to explain complex concepts to non-experts.|Monotonous, overly defensive of academic terminology, or relies on buzzwords.|
