# Camp Audax / The Gathering

## Summary

Camp Audax is Smoking Tigers' camp at The Gathering — a multi-day regenerative community event. STE's role is **not** to design programming. It is to:
1. Coordinate ticket sales (minimum 20 guests)
2. Recruit groups and networks (RMA, ARC, SolarPunks, Q1, PandaBlocks)
3. Produce content artifacts from the event

A second, higher-value opportunity runs in parallel: a **satellite event at The MAB** (North Beach SF) during **Tech Week SF in mid-October**.

---

## The Gathering — Structure

- **Event:** The Gathering (US edition, Camp Navarro, ~20–25 camps planned)
- **Camp model:** Each camp brings minimum 20 guests at $250–$450/ticket
- **Camp host commission:** ~20% of their camp's ticket sales (~$900–$1,700 for 20 tickets)
- **The Gathering keeps:** 80% of ticket revenue for OPEX
- **Internet:** confirmed available at Camp Navarro (Victor)
- **Scale:** 5 camps = 100 people = meaningful revenue
