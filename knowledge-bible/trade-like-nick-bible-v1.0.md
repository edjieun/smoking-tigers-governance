## 0) TL;DR

Trade Like Nick (TLN) is a creator-led options education channel and product business being built with Smoking Tigers Media (STM) using a RevPoints-funded production model. Nick's edge is a "trusted teacher" persona (math teacher + pro trading background) delivering a simple, repeatable options-selling system ("be the casino") to a low-capital audience ($3k–$5k). The immediate bottleneck is consistent recording + a repeatable set/workflow; STM's job is to provide brand, production, and distribution systems so Nick can "show up and record" while the studio handles post + publishing + tracking.

---

## 1) Non-Negotiables

1. **Consistency beats perfection.** Early content can be rough — it must ship so the team can iterate.
2. **One repeatable set + routine.** Home/classroom + controlled lighting/audio. No one-off locations.
3. **Nick buys access to the studio team via RevPoints.** RP are the instrument to pay for support services — not a vague "partnership promise."
4. **Production + distribution must be schedulable + trackable.** Build toward a system with clear accounting and hour tracking.

## 2) Deal Facts (Canonical)

### 2.1 RevPoints Purchase

- Nick paid **$2,500 USD** → receives **50,000 RevPoints**
- RP face value: **$1.00/RP** (standard Q1)
- Sale price: **$0.20/RP** (Nick buys at 20 cents on the dollar)
- Total face value of RP issued: **$50,000**
- Possible second payment: **$2,500 ~1 month later** at same rate
- No forced multiplier — contributors negotiate their own terms

### 2.2 What RP Are For

Nick's RP create runway to pay for production support (editing, brand, ops scheduling) in lieu of cash. Contributors accepting RP choose: market rate, their own multiplier, or cash alternative.

## 4) Audience + Positioning

- **Target:** 18–30, financially curious, starting with $3k–$5k
- **North star:** "Bill Nye meets Options Trading"
- **Nick = trusted teacher:** calm, math-driven, probability-first
- **Core hook:** Selling options = being the casino (vs. buying = gambling)

---

## 5) Content Architecture

### Serialized "Digital University"
Publish as a curriculum: Semester → Classes → numbered lectures. Increases binge behavior + watch time.

### Minimum Viable Backlog (MVB)
Maintain 4 fully edited/scheduled videos ahead at all times. Protects cadence.

### "Curriculum Bridge" CTA
End each video sending viewers directly to the next class — not generic "like/subscribe."
