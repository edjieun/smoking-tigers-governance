## What This Agreement Is

Ed Hwang (operating as Quorum1 LLC d/b/a Smoking Tigers Media Group) and Niels (or Tribre) are agreeing to work together to build the **Smoking Tigers AI MVP**.

This agreement covers three things:
1. **What Niels will build** — specific MVP deliverables defined below
2. **What Niels gets paid** — Rev Points (not cash), tracked in an official ledger
3. **European rights** — Niels/Tribre gets the right to operate ST:AI in Europe once the product is built

---

## The Offer

Smoking Tigers Media Group is offering Niels **[TBD] Rev Points (RP)** for contributing to the defined ST:AI MVP scope, potentially combined with a partial cash component (to be confirmed — see Open Items).

- **1 Rev Point = $1.00 face value**
- Rev Points are not cash and not equity
- They are tracked in an official ledger
- When the ST:AI project ledger reaches 100% (break-even), RP holders can exchange their Rev Points for USD at a rate of **1 RP : $1 USD**
- This applies to **all RP holders on the project equally**, regardless of role
- RPM (multiplier) compensates for time-to-conversion risk at early stage

4.2 **Nature of Rev Points.** Rev Points are an internal trust currency issued under the Q1 Governance Framework. They are:
  - (a) **Not equity** — no ownership or voting rights are conferred
  - (b) **Not wages** — no employment relationship is created
  - (c) **Not guaranteed cash** — recovery is conditional on project revenue
  - (d) **Not securities** — RP do not constitute an investment contract under applicable law

4.3 **Ledger and Recovery.** Rev Points are recorded in the official ST:AI project ledger. When the ledger reaches 100% (the "Break-Even Threshold"), RP holders may exchange Rev Points for USD at a rate of **1 RP : $1.00 USD** through the Q1 currency exchange mechanism. This exchange right applies equally to all RP holders on the project, regardless of role.

4.4 **Progressive Vesting — Milestone-Based.**

| Milestone | Trigger | RP Vesting |
|-----------|---------|------------|
| Milestone 1: Break-Even | Ledger revenue covers market-rate equivalent of contribution | Base RP (1× equivalent) vest |
| Milestone 2: Growth | Revenue exceeds break-even by defined multiplier | RPM-multiplied RP begin vesting |
| Milestone 3: Scale | Sustained revenue / operational maturity | Remaining RPM RP fully vest |

4.5 **Pro-Rata Vesting on Early Exit.** If Contributor exits or this Agreement is terminated prior to full MVP delivery, Rev Points will be issued pro-rata based on milestones completed and accepted by Company.

4.6 **No Clawback.** Once Rev Points are issued and recorded in the Ledger, they will not be clawed back by Company except in cases of material breach or fraud by Contributor.
