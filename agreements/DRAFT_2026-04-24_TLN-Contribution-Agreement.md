# PART I: PLAIN-ENGLISH VERSION

---

## What This Agreement Is

Ed Hwang (operating as Quorum1 LLC d/b/a Smoking Tigers Media Group) and Nick Pham are agreeing on the terms under which Nick earns Rev Points as a Contributor to the Trade Like Nick (TLN) project.

TLN is treated as an **IP Enterprise** — a business asset owned by Smoking Tigers Media Group (STM). Nick is the Creator and key contributor to that asset. This agreement defines how his participation is recognized and compensated through Rev Points.

---

## What Nick Gets

Nick earns **Rev Points (RP)** for completing defined, performance-based contributions to the TLN IP Enterprise. RP are tracked in the official STM Rev Point Ledger.

- RP earning is **performance-based** — tied to completing OKRs and milestones
- RP are **not issued for showing up** — general participation (e.g., recording a video) does not qualify unless it satisfies a defined milestone or is attached to a specific deliverable
- RP earning rate is the **same rate applied to Ed Hwang and Basil Childers** as contributors to TLN
- Specific products (e.g., a Notion Dashboard, a published video series) may have their own RP schedules defined in Exhibit A

---

## What Does NOT Qualify for RP

The following activities do **not** count toward RP earnings by themselves:
- Recording a video that does not meet a defined milestone or deliverable spec
- Attending calls or meetings without an associated deliverable
- General project participation not tied to a defined OKR or product

---

## The IP Asset

All contributions are made to the **Trade Like Nick IP Enterprise** — this includes the brand, content, systems, audience, and commercial infrastructure developed under the TLN project. Nick contributes to this asset; he does not own it through this agreement.

---

## What Nick Does NOT Get

- ❌ No ownership of the TLN IP, Quorum1 LLC, or Smoking Tigers Media Group
- ❌ No equity or profit-sharing outside of the Rev Points ledger
- ❌ No cash through this agreement
- ❌ No guaranteed return — RP recover value from TLN revenue only
- ❌ No governance authority over STM or the TLN enterprise

#### 4.5 Nature of Rev Points

Rev Points are an internal trust currency issued under the Q1 Governance Framework. They are:
- **(a) Not equity** — no ownership or voting rights are conferred
- **(b) Not wages** — no employment relationship is created
- **(c) Not guaranteed cash** — recovery is conditional on TLN project revenue
- **(d) Not securities** — RP do not constitute an investment contract under applicable law

#### 4.6 Recovery — Rev Point Ledger

Rev Points recover value from TLN Enterprise revenue. When the TLN project ledger reaches 100% (the "Break-Even Threshold"), RP holders may exchange Rev Points for USD at a rate of **1 RP : $1.00 USD** through the Q1 currency exchange mechanism. This applies equally to all RP holders on the project.

#### 4.7 Progressive Vesting — Milestone-Based

| Milestone | Trigger | What Vests |
|-----------|---------|------------|
| 1: Break-Even | TLN revenue covers contributor's market-rate value | Base RP (1× equivalent) vest |
| 2: Growth | Revenue exceeds break-even by defined RPM multiplier | RPM-multiplied RP begin vesting |
| 3: Scale | Sustained revenue / operational maturity | Remaining RPM RP fully vest |

#### 4.8 Product-Specific RP

Specific products (e.g., a Notion Dashboard, a published video series, a content module) may carry their own RP schedule. These are defined in **Exhibit A — Product RP Schedule** and require Steward approval before issuance.
