### 1. Purchase of Rev Points

1.1 **Rev Points Issued.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement and the Q1 Governance Framework, the Issuer hereby agrees to issue to Purchaser **12,500 Rev Points (RP)** in the Smoking Tigers Media Group investment ledger (the "Ledger") upon receipt of the Purchase Amount.

1.2 **Purchase Amount.** Purchaser agrees to pay, and Issuer acknowledges receipt of, **$2,500 USD** (the "Purchase Amount") as consideration for the Rev Points issued hereunder.

1.3 **Sale Price and Face Value.** Rev Points are issued at a sale price of **$0.20 per RP** (20% of face value). The face value of each Rev Point remains **$1.00** for recovery and ledger purposes. Nick purchases at a discounted rate providing additional runway to use Rev Points in lieu of cash for production services. The $2,500 purchase price yields 12,500 RP at $0.20/RP.

---

### 2. Nature of Rev Points

2.1 **Not Equity.** Rev Points are not equity, ownership interest, or securities in Quorum1 LLC or Smoking Tigers Media Group. Purchaser acquires no ownership, voting rights, or control over the Company or its operations through this Agreement.

2.2 **Not Wages or Employment.** This Agreement does not create an employment relationship. Rev Points are not wages, salary, or guaranteed compensation.

2.3 **Internal Trust Currency.** Rev Points are an internal mechanism for tracking capital contributions and coordinating participation within the Q1 governance framework. RP remain disconnected from revenue until the moment of currency exchange (RP → USD) as defined by the Q1 governance framework.

2.4 **Revenue Share Mechanism.** Rev Points entitle the holder to participate in revenue share distributions from the Ledger, as defined by the Q1 Governance Framework, when and if revenue is available and when conditions for distribution are met. No distribution is guaranteed.

2.5 **Rev Token Security Layer.** The Rev Token instrument (as defined in the Q1 Governance Framework) governs the mechanics of the exchange process and secures the RP obligation. Rev Tokens are the investment and governance layer; Rev Points are the operational layer.

---

### 3. Governance Framework

3.1 **Governing Documents.** This Agreement is governed by and subject to the Q1 Governance Framework maintained by Quorum1 LLC, including the financial model, investment ledger design, and applicable policies as they exist and are amended from time to time.

3.2 **Q1 Supremacy.** Where conflicts arise between this Agreement or any Smoking Tigers Media Group governing documents and the Q1 Governance Framework, the Q1 Governance Framework controls.

3.3 **Ledger Administration.** Rev Points issued hereunder will be recorded in the STM investment ledger. Administration of the ledger, including revenue share calculations and distribution, is governed by the Q1 Governance Framework and administered by the Steward and Team Finance.
