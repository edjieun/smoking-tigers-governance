## Purpose

This document defines the operational hubs within Smoking Tigers Media's workspace infrastructure. Hubs organize work by role and access tier. 

> ⚠️ **Note:** Hubs are evolving. This document is a living stub — hub definitions will be formally expanded as each hub matures and its operational scope is fully established. Do not treat this as a final or complete specification.

---

## Hubs Overview

STM currently defines four hubs. Not all hubs are fully operational or defined.

---

## Artist/Creator Hub

**Purpose:** Creator/Partner workspace for active project management.

**Contains:**
- Project backlog
- Scheduled sessions
- Deliverables tracking

**Access:** Creator/Partner tier and above (Stewards have full access; Creator/Partners have access within their project scope).

**Status:** Operational (scope evolving).

---

## Producer Hub

**Purpose:** Production contributor workspace for day-to-day production coordination.

**Contains:**
- Session notes
- Content pipeline
- Riverside recordings
- Google Drive content links

**Access:** Assigned production contributors (for their project scope) and Stewards.

**Status:** Operational (scope evolving).

---

## Business Hub

**Purpose:** Business operations workspace covering distribution, sponsorship, and financial records.

**Contains:**
- Distribution tracking
- Sponsor management
- RevPoint accounting
- Financial accounting

**Access:**
- **Stewards:** Full access by default
- **Contributors:** Read-only access to business summaries relevant to their assigned project only. Full access (write access or cross-project visibility) requires explicit Steward grant.
- **Rationale:** Role-based access control prevents interference with financial and distribution records. Write access is restricted to qualified, responsible parties.

**Status:** Operational (scope evolving).

---

## Supporter Hub

**Purpose:** Public-facing forum for Supporters — a space for community engagement, project updates, and supporter interaction. Not an internal operations hub.

**Access:** Open to Supporters and the general public (scoped as determined by Stewards).

**Contents:**
- Project-level updates
- Community announcements
- Supporter resources

**Status:** Basic structure established. Full scoping deferred — details (platform, moderation, content guidelines) to be defined as the Supporter program matures.

---

## Review Cadence

Review as each hub matures. Formal definitions should be established before operational launch of each hub.
