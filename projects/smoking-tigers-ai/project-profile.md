# **Smoking Tigers AI**

**Project Type:** Internal Platform → Productized Service

**Status:** Active (Internal v1)

**Owner / Steward:** Smoking Tigers Media

**Primary User:** STM Leadership (Ed, core stewards)

**Secondary Users (Future):** Creators, Studios, Founder-led teams

---

## **Project Summary**

**Smoking Tigers AI** is a private, local-first executive intelligence system designed to help creative businesses turn messy human work into clear decisions, coordinated action, and durable institutional memory.

It began as an internal operating system for Smoking Tigers Media — supporting leadership, project coordination, governance, and creator operations — and is being intentionally developed as a **productized AI service** for other creator-led teams once proven in real use.

This is not a chatbot.

It is a **human-in-the-loop decision system**.

---

## **The Core Problem It Solves**

Creative organizations don’t fail because of lack of talent.

They fail because:

* Decisions get buried in meetings

* Context lives in people’s heads

* Projects drift without clear ownership

* Tools fragment thinking instead of supporting it

* AI tools talk, but don’t *operate*

Smoking Tigers AI exists to solve **coordination decay** — without sacrificing autonomy, privacy, or governance.

---

## **What Smoking Tigers AI Is**

Smoking Tigers AI is:

* 🧠 A **Chief-of-Staff–style intelligence layer**

* 🗂 A **canonical memory system** for decisions, projects, and roles

* 🧭 A **governance-aware assistant** that respects authority and promotion

* 🛠 A **workflow brain** that prepares actions but never executes unapproved changes

* 🔒 A **private-by-default AI system**, not a cloud data exhaust

It helps leadership *think, decide, and coordinate* — not just generate text.

---

## **What It Is Not**

* ❌ Not a SaaS chatbot

* ❌ Not an automation free-for-all

* ❌ Not an “AI runs your business” fantasy

* ❌ Not dependent on sending data to third-party AI clouds

* ❌ Not a replacement for human judgment

## **Internal Use Case (Product v1)**

Smoking Tigers AI is currently used internally to support:

### **1\. Executive Thinking & Briefings**

* Normalize messy notes into clear options

* Prepare leadership briefs

* Surface risks, blockers, and priorities

### **2\. Meetings → Decisions → Actions**

* Ingest transcripts

* Extract **candidate decisions**

* Draft DACI logs and next steps

* Require human promotion before anything becomes “real”

### **3\. Project Coordination**

* Maintain canonical project profiles

* Track ownership, milestones, and risks

* Prevent drift across multiple creator projects

### **4\. Governance & Memory**

* Separate **draft thinking** from **committed truth**

* Preserve institutional memory in Markdown

* Maintain auditability over time

### **5\. Creator & Client Operations (STM Context)**

* Project intake → structured project profiles

* Proposal and scope drafting

* RevPoints / compensation logic support

* Cross-project visibility without centralization

---

## **System Principles (Non-Negotiable)**

Smoking Tigers AI is built on the following principles:

* **Local-first** (runs on STM-controlled hardware)

* **Human-in-the-loop** (no silent writes, no auto-execution)

* **Governance-aware** (authority, promotion, and ownership matter)

* **Explainable** (why something exists is as important as what exists)

* **Composable** (fits into existing tools like Notion, Slack, Drive)

* **Reversible** (no changes without rollback paths)

---

## **Technical Posture (High Level)**

* Local AI runtime (Ollama)

* Multiple role-specific models (ops, structured output, strategy)

* Canonical knowledge stored in human-readable Markdown

* Notion used as **coordination UI**, not source of truth

* Automation intentionally limited and staged

* No public endpoints, no data leakage

(Details live in the internal system context docs.)
