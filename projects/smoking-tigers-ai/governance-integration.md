## **1\.** 

## **Steward / System Architect (Owner Role)**

**Status:** Required (you)

### **Why this role exists**

This is the *keystone role*. Without it, STM AI collapses into either:

* a chatbot, or

* an over-automated mess.

### **Core responsibilities**

* Own the **governance logic** (what is draft vs truth)

* Define and enforce **human-in-the-loop boundaries**

* Approve decision promotion

* Set RevPoint issuance rules and RPM bands

* Maintain coherence between:

  * product

  * economics

  * charter

  * client delivery

### **Why it cannot be delegated (yet)**

This role requires:

* economic judgment

* governance authority

* product intuition

* trust capital

👉 This is **not** a PM or CTO role.

It’s closer to *chief of staff \+ system designer*.

---

## **2\.** 

## **AI Ops & Prompt Systems Lead**

**Status:** Required (part-time / contributor)

### **Why this role exists**

STM AI’s value does **not** come from models — it comes from:

* predictable outputs

* constrained behavior

* repeatable structures

### **Core responsibilities**

* Build and maintain:

  * Modelfiles

  * prompt templates

  * output schemas (DACI, briefs, project profiles)

* Tune models for:

  * low hallucination

  * structured output

  * governance awareness

* Maintain local-first posture (no cloud creep)

### **Lean note**

* This role is **high leverage**

* Can be:

  * fractional

  * RevPoints-heavy

  * milestone-based

---

## **3\.** 

## **Knowledge & Documentation Curator**

**Status:** Required (lean, ongoing)

### **Why this role exists**

STM AI only works if:

* knowledge is legible

* canonical sources are clean

* drafts don’t leak into truth

### **Core responsibilities**

* Normalize messy inputs into:

  * Markdown

  * canonical docs

* Maintain:

  * project profiles

  * governance docs

  * decision logs

* Enforce documentation standards:

  * naming

  * versioning

  * promotion rules

### **Why this matters**

Without this role:

* AI confidence degrades

* trust erodes

* outputs drift

This role is **anti-chaos**.

---

## **4\.** 

## **Stewardship / Client Onboarding Lead**

**Status:** Required (initially you; later separable)

### **Why this role exists**

STM AI is **not plug-and-play**.

Clients must be:

* oriented

* filtered

* educated

* corrected early

### **Core responsibilities**

* Set expectations:

  * discipline required

  * no autopilot

  * governance matters

* Guide:

  * initial setup

  * early workflows

  * first decision promotions

* Prevent misaligned clients from scaling pain

### **Lean note**

Early on, this role:

* should stay close to the Steward

* protects the brand

* protects the model

---

## **5\.** 

## **RevPoint & Economics Administrator**

**Status:** Required (lightweight, but explicit)

### **Why this role exists**

RevPoints are powerful *only if they are boring and precise*.

### **Core responsibilities**

* Track:

  * RevPoints issued

  * RPM applied

  * recovery progress

* Enforce:

  * $2.4M recovery cap

  * 4.1× RPM max

  * annual recovery throttle

* Maintain transparency for contributors and stewards

### **Lean note**

* This can be:

  * part-time

  * combined with ops

  * tooling-light (spreadsheet \+ discipline)

But it **must exist** as a role.
