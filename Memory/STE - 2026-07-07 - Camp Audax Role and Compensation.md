# Camp Audax Role and Compensation

## Summary
Ed and Christine identified that neither has a formal agreement, defined roles, or compensation structure for Camp Audax work. No formal offer from Victor or The Gathering exists. The camp host economics were mapped out: hosts bring a minimum of 20 guests at $250–$450/ticket, receive ~20% of their camp's ticket sales (~$900–$1,700 for 20 tickets), with The Gathering keeping 80% for OPEX. At scale (5 camps = 100 people), revenue is meaningful. Ed insisted compensation must be formalized before further work proceeds — "If we succeed, are we getting paid for it? What do we want to get out of it?"

## Decisions
- Camp Audax compensation must be formalized before further work: budget line items for Christine's travel, Ed's time, Victor's time.
- Minimum camp host commitment: 20 ticket sales.
- Camp host receives ~20% of their ticket revenue.
- No further one-pager work until compensation clarity is in place.
