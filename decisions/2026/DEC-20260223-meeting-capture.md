# DEC-20260223-meeting-capture

**Decision ID:** DEC-20260223-005
**Title:** Adopt Meeting Capture Policy
**Status:** Approved
**Date:** 2026-02-23
**Owner / Approver:** Ed (Steward)

## Context
Meeting knowledge is often lost across scattered sources (recordings, chat threads, notes, AI summaries). A standardized capture process is needed.

## Decision
Adopt a multi-source meeting capture policy that defines source hierarchy, required outputs, and a phased workflow with human approval gates.

## Rationale
- Ensures decisions are traceable to source artifacts
- Reduces ambiguity in action items and commitments
- Supports OpenClaw-assisted processing with human oversight

## Tradeoffs / Risks
- Adds process overhead to meeting workflows
- Requires discipline from facilitators and note-takers

## Impacts
- All decision-relevant meetings must produce standardized capture packages
- OpenClaw agents may assist but cannot finalize decisions without human approval

## Dependencies
- Shared calendar policy for scheduling integration
- Knowledge staging pipeline for downstream processing
