# DEC-20260315-001 — Frank Anzures Onboarding as Time Investor

**Decision ID:** DEC-20260315-001
**Title:** Frank Anzures Onboarding as Time Investor (Contributor Economic Model)
**Status:** Approved
**Date:** 2026-03-15
**Owner / Approver:** Ed (Steward)

---

## Context

Frank Anzures has been engaged in strategy discussions around ST:AI and the Smoking Tigers venture studio model. A meeting is scheduled for March 16, 2026 to formalize the relationship.

Frank is not coming in as a cash investor. He is a **time investor** — contributing expertise, network, and advisory capacity in exchange for Revenue Points commensurate to the value he commits to bring.

## Decision

**Frank Anzures is invited to join as a time investor in the contributor economic model.** His RP allocation will be determined collaboratively based on the value he commits to contribute (network, advisory capacity, product, ops, or other). There are no special carve-outs — same model as every other contributor.

## Rationale

- The contributor economic model is designed for exactly this: turning non-cash contributions into trackable, redeemable stake
- Frank experiencing the model firsthand is the demonstration of value
- RP are the right instrument: no equity complexity, no securities risk at this stage, clean alignment

## RP Allocation Process

1. Frank articulates the value he commits to bring (Mar 16 meeting)
2. Ed and Frank agree on RP equivalent
3. Frank purchases RP at face value (via Stripe once live; wire/manual in interim)
4. Contribution tracked in governance repo as active time investor
