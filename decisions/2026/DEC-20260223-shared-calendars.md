# DEC-20260223-shared-calendars

**Decision ID:** DEC-20260223-006
**Title:** Adopt Shared Calendars and Meeting Scheduling Policy
**Status:** Approved
**Date:** 2026-02-23
**Owner / Approver:** Ed (Steward)

## Context
Scheduling coordination across a global team requires a consistent calendar architecture and scheduling standard to reduce friction and improve reliability.

## Decision
Adopt a shared calendar policy with separate calendars by function, structured scheduling process, and timezone normalization standards.

## Rationale
- Reduces back-and-forth scheduling coordination
- Supports global collaborators across time zones
- Provides foundation for time/accountability tracking

## Tradeoffs / Risks
- Requires adoption of calendar hygiene practices across the team
- Optional Cal.com upgrade adds tooling complexity

## Impacts
- All recurring operational meetings must live on shared calendars
- Meeting invitations must include standardized metadata

## Dependencies
- Google Calendar as base tooling
- Contributor calendar hour tracking policy
